Inventory & Stock Control

Track stock per branch, catch what is running low, move goods between branches on a challan, replenish from suppliers, and audit every movement — the day-to-day inventory loop in five screens.

1

STEP 01·SEARCH

Check stock levels for the branch

Stock Levels opens on the active branch — Head Office here, costed FIFO in BDT. Scan a barcode or type a SKU to pull its on-hand, reserved, and available quantities instantly.

2

STEP 02·ORIENT

See what to restock next

Low Stock lists every item below its reorder threshold. Needs Reorder counts what to act on now; the Deficit column shows exactly how many units short each SKU is.

3

STEP 03·CLICK

Move stock between branches

Transfers move goods branch-to-branch on a challan. Click New Transfer and follow the head-office flow — create, approve, dispatch. The move decrements the sender and increments the receiver in one step.

4

STEP 04·CLICK

Raise a purchase order

To replenish from a supplier rather than another branch, open Purchase Orders and click New Purchase Order. Received goods post straight into stock and update on-hand quantities.

5

STEP 05·ORIENT

Audit every stock movement

Movements is the full audit trail — every receipt, shipment, transfer, and adjustment with its from-and-to and status. Filter by type, SKU, or date range to trace exactly how a quantity changed.
